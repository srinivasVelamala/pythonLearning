 Types:
 1. Line plot   ex:- plt.plot(x, y, marker='o', color='green', label='Original')
 2. Bar plot ex:- plt.bar(categories, values, color='skyblue')
 3. Scatter plot   ex:- plt.scatter(x, y, color='red', marker='o')
 4. Histograms plot ex:- plt.hist(data, bins=9, color='purple', edgecolor='black')
